## ðŸ“– Understanding the Data

### Scenario Columns
- **scenario_id**: Unique identifier (1, 2, 3...)
- **retirement_age**: Age you retire
- **rrsp_balance**: Starting RRSP amount
- **tfsa_balance**: Starting TFSA amount
- **annual_spending**: Yearly expenses
- **num_pensions**: Number of pension income sources
- **num_properties**: Number of real estate holdings

### Result Columns
- **success**: TRUE = money lasts to age 90
- **final_balance**: Amount left at age 90
- **warnings**: Issues encountered (if any)

### Year-by-Year Data
Each scenario has ~35 rows (age 55-90):
- **year**: Years since start (0, 1, 2...)
- **age**: Your age that year
- **total_balance**: Sum of all accounts
- **gross_income**: Income that year
- **taxes_estimated**: Taxes paid
